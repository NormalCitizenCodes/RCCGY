The Legacy of RC CGY

"A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step, but it's finished with a crew."

The Spark

In the quiet streets of Puntod, Cagayan de Oro, a small group of strangers found a common rhythm. What began as a humble effort to stay active evolved into the grit of finishing the next kilometer together. We didn't just start a club; we sparked a change.

The Movement

Today, RC CGY is a synchronized movement. We have transformed the pavement into a stage for excellence, bringing together athletes, dreamers, and partners. From the first light of dawn to the city's night glow, our presence is felt across the city.

Lace Up Your Legacy

Growth happens outside the comfort zone. Whether you are running your first block or your tenth marathon, your presence makes us stronger. Don't just watch the movement—be the movement.
